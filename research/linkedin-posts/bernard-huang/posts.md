## Post 2: "How AEO / GEO / AI SEO Actually Works"
**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/bernardjhuang_how-aeo-geo-ai-seo-actually-works-with-activity-7404909477218426880-c7nB  
**Theme:** AEO mechanics

**Key excerpt:**
> "The 3 layers of AI-driven search: 1) Training data — what was in the model's training corpus (you can't change this retroactively). 2) AI validation searches — when an AI does real-time web searches to check its answer, your content needs to rank for those queries. 3) Memory/context — when users have cited your brand in past sessions. The real goal of AEO is to win Layer 2: earn citations from real-time web searches that AI tools perform."

**Why this matters:** The most technically precise explanation of how AEO actually functions — essential for building a content strategy that works with AI answer engines, not just Google.
