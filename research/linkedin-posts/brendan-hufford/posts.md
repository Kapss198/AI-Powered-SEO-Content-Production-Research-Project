## Post 3: "From SEO to AEO: The B2B Shift"
**Theme:** AEO vs SEO strategy  
**Collected from:** Brendan Hufford LinkedIn, 2025

**Key excerpt:**
> "B2B SaaS SEO has always been about capturing demand. AEO is about shaping demand. SEO: buyer types 'best project management tool' → your article appears → they click. AEO: buyer asks ChatGPT 'what project management tool should a 30-person remote team use?' → AI cites your brand in the answer → buyer never clicks. The AEO play isn't about traffic. It's about brand mentions in AI-generated recommendations. Completely different metric."

**Why this matters:** Reframes the B2B content goal from traffic generation to brand citation — fundamental shift in how to measure AI content production success.
