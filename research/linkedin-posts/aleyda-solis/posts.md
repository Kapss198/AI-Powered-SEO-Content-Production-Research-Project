## Post 2: "The AI Search Content Optimization Checklist"
**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/aleyda_the-ai-search-content-optimization-checklist-activity-7340439923935563776-A66c  
**Theme:** Practical AEO checklist

**Key excerpt:**
> "If you want your content to be cited by AI search engines, you need: ✓ Clear, direct answers to specific questions at the top of the page ✓ Structured content with proper headers ✓ Factual, well-sourced, up-to-date information ✓ Author authority signals (bio, credentials, links to their profiles) ✓ Entity mentions that connect your brand to key topics ✓ Coverage of the full topic, not just the head keyword ✓ Content that is already cited or linked to by trusted sources"

**Why this matters:** Operational checklist — maps directly to what an AI-powered content production team should optimize for in every article.
